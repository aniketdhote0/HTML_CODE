BOLLYWOOD MOVIES

Bollywood refers to the Hindi-language film industry based in Mumbai (formerly Bombay), India. It is one of the largest and most prolific film industries in the world. Bollywood movies are known for their vibrant storytelling, song-and-dance sequences, and a mix of drama, romance, comedy, and action.

1. Romance: Often central to Bollywood films, with iconic movies like Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge and Kuch Kuch Hota Hai.
2. Action: Features larger-than-life heroes and thrilling fight sequences, e.g., sholay, Dabangg.

Popular Bollywood Actors

 1. Amitabh Bachchan:Iconic star known as the "Shahenshah of Bollywood."
2. Shah Rukh Khan: The "King of Romance," with blockbusters like Dil Se, My Name is Khan.
3. Salman Khan: Known for his action-packed roles in Bajrangi Bhaijaan, Sultan.
4. Aamir Khan: The "Perfectionist," famous for films like Lagaan, Dangal.

Popular Directors

 1. Yash Chopra: Known for romantic classics.
2. Karan Johar: Famous for family dramas and romantic films.
3. Rajkumar Hirani: Master of combining humor and social messages (3 Idiots,Pk).
4. Sanjay Leela Bhansali: Known for visually opulent films (Devdas, Gangubai Kathiawadi).

Music in Bollywood

 Music is integral to Bollywood films. Renowned composers like A.R. Rahman, Shankar-Ehsaan-Loy, and Pritam have created evergreen songs. Singers like Lata Mangeshkar, Kishore Kumar, Arijit Singh, and Shreya Ghoshal are celebrated for their contributions.

Milestones in Bollywood

 1. First Bollywood Film: Raja Harishchandra (1913), a silent film by Dadasaheb Phalke.
2. First Sound Film: Alam Ara (1931), the first Indian talkie.
3. Global Recognition: Films like Slumdog Millionaire and RRR brought international acclaim.
4. Longest-running Movie: Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge (1995), still shown in Mumbai theaters.
